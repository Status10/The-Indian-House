Our Specials!

Matar Keema

Mattar Keema, known globally as Minced Meat, includes ingredients such as potatoes, ginger, chili, onion, butter and several spices. Coming from the Indian Subcontinent, it was popularly eaten in the courts of Mughal India. They were served in special occasions, like weddings and celebrations.

Masala Dosa

Dosa is a pancake made from rice flour and ground pulses. It's made by soaking rice and lentils overnight in water and then griding it into a batter.

Mughlai Chicken

Mughlai Chicken is made up of Butter, onions, nuts, garlic, green chili, Chicken and ginger.
Beware, they can get quite spicy! They're made of pure Indian spices.

Kati Rolls

A street food from India, it's a roasted kebab wrapped around a paratha (which you can find out about here) With kebabs, they also include egg. (though not in every roll)

Butter Chicken

One of the most famous Indian dishes, it's a type a chicken with a curry made with butter and spiced tomato. The sauce has a very rich texture. The Chicken is marinated for hours, with a mixture of Lemon juice, yogurt, salt, Kashmiri Red Chili and a garlic paste.

Biryani

Another one of the most prominent Indian dishes. It's made with famous Indian Spices, rice and chicken. Some additions include eggs. Along with India, it spread to other countries on Earth, like Iraq, Malaysia and Thailand.
